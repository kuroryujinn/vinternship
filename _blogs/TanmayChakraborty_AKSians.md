### Five weeks ago, I received an email.

An opportunity to intern and learn through ViBe — the virtual internship platform by IIT Ropar. I didn't know then how much this one email would change the way I think about learning itself.
Learning That Doesn't Let You Sleep Through It
We've all been there. You open a YouTube tutorial, watch it passively, maybe copy some code along the way — and by the end, you've "completed" it but understood very little. The topic never truly became yours.
---
ViBe was different from day one.
Right after every tutorial, there were quizzes — not as a formality, but as a mirror. They forced me to ask: Did I actually understand this, or did I just watch it? That single design choice changed everything. The content was carefully curated, and it showed. This wasn't an accident — this was the vision of Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar sir, and the thoughtfulness behind every module reflected exactly that.
The Riddle of Vikramaditya — and This Aditya
One thing this course teaches you quietly, without making a big announcement about it, is CONSISTENCY.
Everyone has heard that word. Posters, Instagram quotes, motivational videos — it's everywhere. But here, consistency didn't feel like a burden. It felt like a riddle. Like the legendary tales of Raja Vikramaditya, who faced challenge after challenge not out of obligation but because the journey itself pulled him forward.
The same feeling was shared — by this Aditya. 😄
The course was structured in a way that it never felt like hard work. It felt like something you wanted to solve. And that, I believe, is the mark of truly great design.
An Instructor Who Makes the Difficult Feel Natural
Nandan Prabhudesai sir taught beautifully.
I've attempted quizzes before — in college, on platforms, during tests — and they've often felt disconnected from what was actually taught. Here, that gap didn't exist. His way of explaining concepts made the quizzes feel like a natural next step, not an obstacle. Teaching done right doesn't just transfer information — it builds intuition. And that's exactly what happened here.
VIDYA — Because Learning Is Not Just Content
You might finish the ViBe course and think — that's it, the content is over. But that's where you'd be wrong.
Because learning here isn't just content. It's VIDYA.
---
The live sessions by Riya Mehta mam are where this YATRA — this journey — truly comes alive. These sessions don't just revise what you've learned. They guide you forward, clear the fog, and remind you that someone is genuinely invested in your growth.
A Community That Words Can't Fully Capture — But I'll Try
The most unexpected gift of this internship has been the community.
---
Where do I even begin?
With Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar sir — whose vision set the tone for everything. With Jinal Gupta mam, Sakshi Sharma mam, Rohit Sharma sir, and Nitin Sankar sir — for always showing up, always interacting, always genuinely asking about the Vinternship journey of every single intern. This community is the kind that stays with you long after the internship ends.
Where I Stand Today
The ViBe course — done. ✅
The case studies — almost there. 🔄
The project — being prepared for, with everything this journey has built in me. 🚀
Five weeks in, and I'm not the same person who opened that email. The code is just one part of it. The bigger shift has been in how I learn, how I show up, and how I think.
Grateful for this opportunity. Grateful for every mentor, every quiz, every live session, and every moment of confusion that eventually turned into clarity.
This is just the beginning. 🙏
