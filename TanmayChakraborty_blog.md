# Five Weeks. One Email. A Shift in How I Learn.

Five weeks ago, I received an email.  
An opportunity to intern and learn through **ViBe** — the virtual internship platform by IIT Ropar.  

I didn't know then how much this one email would change the way I think about learning itself.

---

## Learning That Doesn't Let You Sleep Through It

We've all been there.  
You open a YouTube tutorial, watch it passively, maybe copy some code along the way — and by the end, you've "completed" it but understood very little. The topic never truly became yours.

**ViBe was different from day one.**

Right after every tutorial, there were quizzes — not as a formality, but as a mirror.  
They forced me to ask:

> *Did I actually understand this, or did I just watch it?*

That single design choice changed everything.

The content was carefully curated, and it showed.  
This wasn't accidental — it reflected the vision of **Prof. Sudarshan Iyengar sir**, visible in every module.

---

## The Riddle of Vikramaditya — and This Aditya 😄

One thing this course teaches you quietly, without making a big announcement about it, is:

> **CONSISTENCY**

We've all heard that word — posters, Instagram quotes, motivational videos.  
But here, it didn't feel like pressure. It felt like a **riddle**.

Like the tales of **Raja Vikramaditya**, facing challenges not out of obligation, but because the journey itself pulled him forward.

And somehow, that same feeling was shared — by this *Aditya*. 😄

The course never felt like hard work.  
It felt like something you *wanted* to solve.

That’s not luck. That’s **great design**.

---

## An Instructor Who Makes the Difficult Feel Natural

**Nandan Prabhudesai sir** taught beautifully.

Most quizzes feel disconnected from what you learn.  
Here, that gap didn’t exist.

His teaching made quizzes feel like a **natural next step**, not a hurdle.

> Good teaching doesn’t just transfer information — it builds intuition.

And that’s exactly what happened.
